HEALTH BENEFITS OF A WALK-IN SHOWERS

  • Warm & Comfortable
    You can remain warm in a walk-in shower since the water only comes in when you turn it on. Waiting for the tub to fill or drain is unnecessary.
  • Added Security
    If you use a walker or a wheelchair, a walk-in shower is a convenient option since it's simple to enter and exit. It's great for everyone, from babies to the elderly to those with disabilities.
  • Improved Personal Hygiene
    There is nothing more relaxing than taking a shower while sitting on the shower bench. Mold and mildew may be prevented by quickly rinsing soap residue from the surface.
  • Long-Term Durability
    Compared to a standard bathtub or shower, walk-in showers have fewer pieces and hence have fewer parts that might break.
  • Reduce Your Water Consumption
    Taking a shower requires 10 to 25 gallons of water, while a bath might use up to 70 gallons. Your water costs might go down by a significant amount!
  • Dementia-Friendly
    Dementia may make people with the condition more resistant to bathing, so a walk-in shower is a good option since it's simple to use and lets you adjust the temperature.
